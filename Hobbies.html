In recent years, I have taken up running as a way to challenge myself both physically and mentally. Strathcarron Hospice is a charity that provides expert **palliative care and support** to people living with life-limiting conditions. They offer specialist care to ensure that patients can **live life to the fullest**, right up until the end.

Their services include **in-patient care, day services, community nursing, and bereavement support** for families. The hospice relies heavily on donations and fundraising events like the **Strathcarron 10K Race** to continue providing these essential services.

You can learn more about **Strathcarron Hospice** and support their cause by visiting their official website: Strathcarron Hospice .
